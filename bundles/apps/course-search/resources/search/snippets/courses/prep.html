Southern Cross University is renowned for its highly supportive environment and has years of experience in supporting students to become confident, independent learners. We aim to bring the opportunity of university study to as many people as possible through preparation courses offered by SCU College.

  • Preparing for Success at SCU Program, offered on campus or by distance education, is an intensive 12-week program to equip students with academic skills appropriate for University.

International students

SCU College helps prepare international students for undergraduate and postgraduate courses at Southern Cross University. Our fully accredited preparation programs include both English language and academic content.

  • English Language Programs for International students offer a diverse suite of General English (GE) and English for Academic Purposes (EAP) courses to equip students with English language and academic skills for university study. Short educational study tours at Coffs Harbour, Gold Coast and Lismore campuses are also available.

Other academic preparation programs include:

  • Undergraduate Qualifying Program
  • Postgraduate Qualifying Program
  • Career Start in Science (Study Abroad)
  • Career Start in Business (Study Abroad)

Please note that course-based programs are available only to students on a student visa.
